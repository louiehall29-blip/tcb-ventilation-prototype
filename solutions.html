Certification

Certificates We Provide

Gas
Gas Safe Certificate

Issued on completion of all gas work by our Gas Safe registered engineers. Legally required for gas installations and appliance work.

Gas
CP12 Landlord Safety

Annual Landlord Gas Safety Record — a legal requirement for all rented residential and commercial properties with gas appliances.

Electrical
EICR

Electrical Installation Condition Report — assessing the safety of your existing electrical installation. Required for landlords every 5 years.

Electrical
EIC / Minor Works

Electrical Installation Certificate or Minor Electrical Works Certificate issued on completion of new electrical installation work.

Fire
BS 5839 Certificate

Fire alarm commissioning certificate to BS 5839 standards — issued on handover of every newly installed or upgraded fire alarm system.

Plumbing
Water Regs Certificate

Water Regulations compliance certificate issued for notifiable plumbing work — confirming the installation meets legal standards.
